• About the game

Hello, and welcome to our game "Nature Sudoku" in this version of the game we decided to make the game a little bit different from the traditional sudoku games we're used to. As well known this kind of game requires concentration with a lot of focus, so to increase concentration to a higher level we added two things wich are very helpful in making the player think more efficiently and rapdly, while also creating a relaxing atmosphere to think clearly - using the relaxing music in the background with extraordinary calming pictures as well.

Some instruction you may need :
1- toggling the music on or off : at the top left corner of the page you have a volume button, pressing it turns the sounds on or off on accordingly
2- changing the background: at the top right corner of the page you have a menu button, when pressing it gives you the "change background" option. You can press it and it well show you a several types of backgrounds which you can choose the one you prefer.

Some informations about the editors: we are Mahmoud Kharbat and Dotan two front-end developers, we learn at SVCOLLEGE a front-end course, we developed this site as part of different projects that we're tasked to do. We hope that you enjoyed visiting our website and playing our "Nature Sudoku" game.
